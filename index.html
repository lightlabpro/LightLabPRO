What is Light Lab PRO?

A complete lighting toolkit inside Unity

Light Lab PRO helps you manage lighting in games, film, visualization, and 3D applications with studio rigs, reusable presets, reactive effects, animated cookies, and time-of-day control.

Studio Lighting

Design a full studio rig around a point of interest with ring-style controls, live gizmos, labels, and flexible key/fill/rim layouts.

  • POI-centric layout with live gizmos and labels
  • Spread, arc, random variation, and arrangement guides
  • Scene and selection toolbars for quick updates
Buy on Unity Asset Store

Presets

Assign a preset asset to a light and update type, color, intensity, range, shadows, effects, and cookies instantly across your project.

  • One preset can drive many lights with perfect consistency
  • Swap moods by reassigning assets, not hunting properties
  • Non-destructive iteration with version-controlled assets
Buy on Unity Asset Store

FX & Settings

Edit lights in context with scene lists, basic settings, color switching, sound-driven effects, sequencers, volumetrics, and material changes.

  • Gradual, strobe, and step sequencer effects
  • Sound Effect Handler and Sound Reactor integration
  • Color Switcher, volumetrics, and Material Changer
Buy on Unity Asset Store

PRO Cookies

Break up light with texture, animated shader, or video cookies — perfect for gobos, firelight, TV spill, and complex projected motion.

Texture

Pan, rotate, scale, and animate static gobos with believable motion.

Animated

Drive cookies from animated shaders for caustics, flicker, and looping patterns.

Video

Project real footage for TVs, clubs, city reflections, and cinematic spill.

Animation

Bring lights to life with Rotator Mode for sweeps and spins, or Firefly Mode for organic wandering motion inside a generated volume.

  • Forward, ping-pong, and random rotation modes
  • World/local axes with speed, jitter, and range controls
  • Firefly motion areas with automatic setup

Day / Night Cycle

Drive a full sky cycle with editor-friendly time controls, directional sun color, and a dedicated moon render feature for clean URP nights.

  • Scrub time with a normalized slider and preview in Edit Mode
  • Directional light color and intensity per time of day
  • Moon renderer for controllable night visualization
