Why Light Lab PRO

A calmer way to direct scene lighting.

Instead of jumping between scattered Unity panels and custom scripts, Light Lab PRO gives lighting decisions a single place to live. Build rigs, reuse looks, animate light behavior, and keep art direction consistent.

Features

Six modules, one lighting workflow.

01

Studio Lighting

Build key, fill, rim, and custom rigs around a point of interest with live gizmos and arrangement controls.

02

Presets

Save complete light looks as reusable assets so scenes stay consistent and easy to iterate.

03

FX & Settings

Shape intensity, range, color, strobe behavior, sound reaction, sequencing, materials, and volumetrics.

04

PRO Cookies

Use texture, animated shader, and video cookies for gobos, caustics, firelight, TV spill, and projected motion.

05

Animation

Add rotator sweeps or organic firefly movement without wiring up custom motion tools from scratch.

06

Day / Night Cycle

Preview time of day in the editor, drive the directional light, and render a controllable moon in URP.
